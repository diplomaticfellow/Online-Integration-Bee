Welcome to the Online Integration Bee, an exclusive event for high school students! We are excited to gather young and motivated mathematical minds from around the world for a unique and exhilarating competition. OIB is designed to celebrate the beauty and importance of calculus while fostering a spirit of friendly rivalry among students.

The goal of this event is to ignite a passion for mathematics and showcase the elegance of integration. Whether you're just beginning your journey into advanced mathematics or you're already a seasoned competitor, OIB offers a challenging and rewarding experience for all high school participants.
